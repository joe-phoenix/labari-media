Who We Are

Independent media,
built for a continent on the move.

Labari Media is a Pan-African independent media group dedicated to journalism, technology intelligence, and data-driven storytelling. We exist because Africa deserves more than headlines — it deserves context, depth, and a platform that treats its audience as the sophisticated readers they are.

Across our brands, we cover the people driving African innovation, the governments shaping policy, the cultural currents, and the data that makes sense of it all.
